Americano

Water and coffee. As an improvement/variation of espresso. Said to be invented in WWII by American troops.
  • Ingredients
    • Coffee
    • Water
    • Ration: 1:12
    • (optional, just if you want to make ice coffee) ice cubes
  • Tastes
    • Not as strong as espresso. Personally speaking it is not that aromatic.
    Ice Americano is nice.
